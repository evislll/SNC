Strategic Negotiations

Achieving individual interest through maintaining lasting partnership

Outcomes

Members can achieve personal goals through developing new skills in their professional lives.

Discovering hidden talents and perspectives, while identifying areas for personal growth.

Collaboration within the community, fostering long-lasting relationships.

Positive impact on society by collaborating and driving meaningful change.

Rules

Often we are faced with challenging situations. Usually, each entails participants and their competing or overlapping interests.

One of the situations is chosen by the host. Each participant speaks out during negotiations, pursuing a certain resolution. Judges can assess by the end of the time which side of the negotiation was more effective.
